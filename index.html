About Me

A Quick Insight Into My Life

My name is Joseph Soria, and I was born in Katy, Texas. I studied Mechanical Engineering at the University of Houston, and I currently work in the Oil and Gas industry. My experience is diverse, as I have worked in Quality, Engineering, and Supply Chain, while interacting with all of these groups and more. In my current position, I have learned how business works in a development, procurement, manufacturing, and operational standpoint. In the future, I see myself moving from Oil and Gas and branching out into Full Stack Web Development with the goal to deploy both web and mobile applications.

I'm currently undertaking a bootcamp from Rice University to continue my education and eventually make a career change; this bootcamp will make me Employer-competitive as I learn skills such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, and much more. As of now, I have used different technologies, such as Node.js, Express.js, Sequelize for ORM in MySQL, and Handlebars.js. I now have a couple of projects that use these technologies to build a full stack application! These projects are showcased here.

Living and Education

As I mentioned before, I was born in Katy, Texas. However, I have moved around to different areas on the west side of Houston. I began to move around in 2013, and have been moving since. I currently live in Cypress, which was the starting point of my commute to school in my undergrad days.

In 2012, I began my studies in the Lone Star College System, where I completed all my basic courses before starting at a four-year university. Years later (2015 to be exact), I transferred to the University of Houston, where I pursued a degree in Mechanical Engineering. I graduated in 2018 with a GPA of 3.33, and undertook the Fundamentals of Engineering exam (FE exam for short). I had passed the exam and became a certified E.I.T, or Engineer in Training, while joining the American Society of Mechanical Engineers.

Enter the year 2020, where I have once again continued to further my education with the Full Stack Flex bootcamp, in partnership with Rice University. Here, I aim to learn and hone skills in multiple languages, with HTML, CSS, and Javascript only being a few of the skills I will learn in this course. Eventually, I will pursue a MBA degree to add to my arsenal.
